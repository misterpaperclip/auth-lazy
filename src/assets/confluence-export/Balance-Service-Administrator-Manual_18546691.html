The "New Account" command will allocate new account.

Security

The "Security" tab makes it possible to check password of the account and to change the password of the account, as well.

  • Password must be rather complex: at least 5 characters long and containing at least two of three character types (lowercase, uppercase, or digits). The maximum password length is 15 characters.

  • Once the password is changed, the connection of the account to the trader server is reset. A reconnection with the new password is required.

Swap Type

  • the type of rollover calculation:

  • by points — swaps are charged in points in the volume specified below for short and long positions. The specified number of points is charged for each lot of a position. Swap size is converted from points to money (deposit currency). To do it, the point price of the corresponding buy position position is calculated (regardless of the actual direction of the position).

  • by money — in calculation currency. The swap size is converted from the base currency into the deposit currency using the average price at the moment of swap charging: (Bid+Ask)/2.

  • by interest — as annual interest rate.  Since swaps are calculated and charged every day at the end of day time, the calculated amount of the annual interest rate is divided by 360. When charging swaps, first the cost of one symbol lot is calculated (the symbol of the opened position), and then the specified percent is calculated, the obtained amount is multiplied by the position volume (in lots) and the result is divided by 360. If the base currency of the symbol is different from the deposit currency, the swap is converted into the deposit currency. The conversion is performed using the average price at the moment of swap charging: (Bid+Ask)/2.

Swap Long

rollover values for calculation of long positions. The values can be redefined.

Swap Short

rollover values for calculation of short positions. The values can be redefined.

Sessions

The Sessions tab is used for controlling the operating time of a financial instrument on certain days of the week.

To set the allowed trading time for the symbol, check the Day in checkbox. Then set the start and end of the period in the "Opening" and "Closing" fields.

Attention: It is highly recommended to perform operations of changing the list of financial instruments on holidays only, when markets are closed.

Journals

All operations performed and all events are stored in special statistical logs representing text files and being kept in the /logs directory. The "Journal" section allows to look through these records. Here you can also view the logs of the platform's service components. User may also request logs on specific time and specific text on log string using “From”, “To”, and “Filter”.

Groups

All users of the system are divided into groups. There are no users on the server who would not belong to a group:

“Added New Group” will give the user the ability to create new group in the platform.

Field

Description

Data Type

Required

Group

This refers to the specific and distinct name assigned to a particular user group. Each user group is identified by a unique name that distinguishes it from other groups within a particular system or platform. This name is used for various purposes, including group management, permissions, and access control.

String

Company

This refers to the specific company or organization that provides services to the user group. The company may offer various services, such as trading, investment management, or financial advice, and may be responsible for managing the group's assets and investments. The name of the company is important as it helps to identify the provider of these services and establishes a level of trust and credibility.

String

MC/SO

These levels refer to specific thresholds that determine the point at which a trader's account may be subject to a Margin Call or Stop Out. A Margin Call is a request for additional funds to be deposited into the account to meet the minimum margin requirement, while a Stop Out is the automatic closure of a trader's positions due to insufficient funds in the account. The Margin Call and Stop Out levels are critical for traders to be aware of as they help to manage risk and prevent substantial losses.

String

Securities

This refers to the inventory of financial instruments or assets that are accessible to the user group for trading or investment purposes. The list of securities may include stocks, bonds, futures, options, and other financial products, depending on the group's investment strategy and objectives. Access to a diverse range of securities is crucial for achieving investment goals and managing risk.

String

Field

Description

Data Type

Required

Group Name

This refers to a specific and distinct name assigned to a user group that differentiates it from other groups within a system or platform. The user group name is an essential identifier used for various purposes, including group management, permissions, and access control. It is crucial for effective group management and helps to maintain order and organization within a trading or investment platform.

Currency

This refers to the default currency used for deposits in the user group. The default currency must be in Latin characters only, without any punctuation marks, spaces, or special characters. The option to change the default currency cannot be modified when the server is operating, provided at least one client is included in the group. This requirement is critical for maintaining consistency in the group's accounting and financial reporting.

String

Margin Call level

This refers to a predefined threshold that alerts traders when their account balance falls below a certain level. The warning level is set to prevent traders from incurring substantial losses and to prompt them to take corrective actions. The warning level is crucial for managing risk and helps traders to stay informed about their account status.

Integer

Sop Out level

This refers to a predefined threshold that triggers the automatic closure of a trader's open positions when their account balance falls below a specific level. The position removal level is set to prevent traders from incurring significant losses beyond their account balance. This level is critical for managing risk and helps traders to avoid unwanted losses.

Integer

in (percent/money)

This refers to the formula used to calculate the Margin Call (MC) level, which is a threshold that alerts traders to add more funds to their account to maintain their open positions. The Margin Call level is calculated as a percentage of equity to margin or is equal to equity in monetary terms. This formula is crucial for managing risk and helps traders to avoid incurring substantial losses.

String

Use Swap

This refers to the option available to traders to enable or disable charging swaps and interests for free margins in their account. The free margin refers to the funds available in the account that can be used for opening new positions. Traders can choose to enable or disable charging swaps and interests for these funds depending on their investment strategy and objectives.

Boolean

Auto-deal

This refers to the possibility for traders to conduct trades outside the platform. This can occur when traders have agreements with third-party brokers or other financial institutions to conduct trades outside the platform. It is important to note that trades conducted outside the platform may not be subject to the same regulations and protections as trades conducted within the platform. It is crucial for traders to be aware of the risks and benefits of conducting trades outside the platform.

Boolean

Holidays

This section allows to introduce holidays to the working schedule both for each group of instruments (forex, cfd, futures, stocks), and for each individual instrument (EURUSD, USDCHF, #GOLD, etc.). These instructions allow to configure holidays during the workweek easily. On holidays, clients can connect to the system, watch charts and bid histories, but they cannot perform any trade operations.

“Add New Holiday” will give access to the user to create new Holiday.

When adding a holiday or changing the rules of work on that day (the "Add" è "Edit" commands respectively) you will see the setting window:

Add New Holiday

This tab is intended to add new holiday in the platform. Its required to fill all the parameters in this tab to be able to create one.

To enable/disable the holiday check/unchek the "Enable" box. Tick the "Every Year" field if the holiday repeats every year. You should indicate the year for other holidays. If market works for several hours on a holiday, you should indicate the working hours in the "Work time" additionally. The name of a financial instrument or that of a group of instruments will be stored in the "Security" field ("All" means all Security), and holiday — will be described in the "Description" field.

Quotes transmission and trading continues until the last minute of the work time range inclusive. For example, if the work time is from 00:00 to 18:00, then the actual work time is 00:00:00 — 18:00:59.

Update Holiday

This tab is intended to modify and update existing holiday.
